What We Do

Our Services

01

Architectural Design

Designing spaces that are functional while creating a luxurious experience, ensuring thoughtful planning, aesthetics, and comfort for modern residential and commercial environments.

02

Redevelopment Planning

Revitalization of existing and aging structures by improving infrastructure, upgrading facilities, and introducing modern amenities to enhance usability and long-term value.

03

Project Management

Managing projects from planning through execution, including coordination between consultants and clients to ensure smooth processes, efficiency, and timely delivery.

04

Statutory Approvals

Handling approvals from various departments and preparing technical drawings for building layouts, including BPMS submissions and physical files.

05

Techno-Legal Consultancy

Providing techno-legal support including 7/12 extract work, NA procedures, legal consultancy for turnkey projects, documentation, and feasibility analysis.

06

Surveys and NOCs

Conducting physical site and drone surveys while obtaining MPCB CTE & CTO, fire NOC, irrigation NOC, environmental compliance, and other required permissions.
